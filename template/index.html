Mission Statement

Blockchain at Columbia is Columbia University's organization for distributed ledger technology. B@C nurtures development in the blockchain ecosystem upon a foundation for technical problem-solving. We seek to serve both Columbia’s campus and the greater New York area and look to foster relevant academic research through critical analysis of new technologies. The organization’s high standard for knowledge encourages both education on complex subjects and distribution of valuable resources on all related topics.
